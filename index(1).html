The Problem

Three structural failures in home ownership

01

Information Failure

Most owners lack accurate, technical understanding of their home's condition and degradation timelines. The result: expensive surprises.

02

Timing Failure

Maintenance is typically reactive, not preventive — resulting in higher-cost interventions. Deferred maintenance accumulates until it becomes a capital emergency.

03

Execution Failure

Even when issues are identified, homeowners struggle to coordinate reliable contractor execution. Fragmented vendors, inconsistent quality, no accountability.
